APPLY TO FUTURE NIGERIA




Application to the Future Nigeria project team is very competitive at the moment. A maximum of 292 Ambassadors who are Nigerians and are within the ages of 15-49 is expected to be admitted to the Future Nigeria Project team following the decision of the Africa Union and this is done in order for applicant to prove to us that they are qualified to be part of the future of this country in whatever areas that they feel that they can. For any applicant who wants to apply to the future Nigeria project team and they feel that they are qualified to be a part of the team please kindly send an email to applyfuturenigeria@gmail.com and make the subject of the email Request for application and send so that you will receive a response from the application committee the form and all other document containing the application. Deadline for all applicant to apply to future Nigeria is 29th June 2018 by 11:59pm Nigerian time. Other ways of how you can be part of the future Nigeria team will be communicated later after application deadline and we have selected the people that we feel that is qualified for the project.
